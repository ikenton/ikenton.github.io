Love Quest is an RPG created by the students of the CPP Game Development Club. Where the player must try to repair their toxic relationship or leave their partner. I created the UI for the quick time event "battles" where the player must avoid projectiles in order to succeed.

Recipe Book Mobile App

Summary

An Android App that allowed a user to store recipes on their phone. They could add, delete, and even upload recipes from the internet. Users can add tags to recipes in order to filter and search for specific meals.

My Contributions

I programmed the basic layout of the list of recipies and the recipe instructions, as well as created the functionality for creating and editting a recipe.

No Leg Up

No Leg Up is a 2D platformer where the player must change the gravity to move through a dangerous obstacle course. I designed the layout for the main menu and the how to play screen.
